Aurelion Sol

Lore

In League of Legends lore Aurelion Sol has the monicker of "the star forger" with his identity being that he is a dragon who wandered the cosmos creating planets and stars before being tricked and having his power stolen by the curse placed upon the crown he wears.

In Game

Within the game our space dragon has the classification of a battle mage with his abilities being as follows:

  1. Passive: Stars orbit Aurelion Sol, dealing magic damage when they hit an enemy.
  2. Q:Aurelion Sol creates an expanding disk, which explodes to stun and damage enemies when it moves too far away from him.
  3. W:Aurelion Sol pushes his stars farther out, increasing their damage and speed.
  4. E:Aurelion Sol takes off flying for a long distance.
  5. R:Aurelion Sol projects a blast of pure starfire, damaging and slowing all enemies caught in it and knocking nearby enemies back to a safer distance.
